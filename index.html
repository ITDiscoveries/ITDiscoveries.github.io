About

Hi, My name is Colin Adreani

I am a student at the John Martinson Honors College at Purdue studying Aerospace Engineering, and, this summer, I will be going on a study away trip called “Italian Discoveries.” Though my continental Italian heritage is distant (A few generations back) I established a link to it through culinary exploration and actual exploration through the tuscan countryside.

I see Italy as a very important part of our collective history; it is the birthplace of the Roman Empire, the cradle renaissance, and the center of the catholic world. Its cultural exports are seen around the world in fashion, cuisine, and music. I believe that this is not coincidental, but rather it is the result of a centuries long history of celebrating and supporting talent when it arises. I hope to learn more about how this attitude developed through history and especially during the turning point that was the renaissance.
